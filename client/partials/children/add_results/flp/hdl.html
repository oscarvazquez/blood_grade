HDL

High levels linked to a reduced risk of heart and blood vessel disease. The higher your HDL level, the better. Cholesterol is a type of fat, found in your blood. It is produced by your body and also comes from the foods you eat (animal products). Cholesterol is needed by your body to maintain the health of your cells.
